Phone Database

Check your lead lists against the LLG phone number database before enriching.

▼ How it works

LLG has 460,000+ contacts with phone numbers collected across all clients. Before you pay to enrich a new lead list, use this tool to check which contacts we already have numbers for — so you only enrich what's actually needed.

Step 1: Check your list

  1. Sign in with your @lynnleadgen.com Google account
  2. Paste the Google Sheet URL of the lead list you want to check
  3. Select the client (for DNC filtering) and click Check List
  4. The tool creates a new Google Sheet with three tabs:
    • Already Have — we have phone numbers for these contacts
    • Need Enrichment — no phone number on file, these need scraping
    • DNC Blocked — on the client's Do Not Contact list, skip these

Step 2: Enrich the gaps

Take the "Need Enrichment" tab and run it through your enrichment source (Apollo (Roy), Clay, Wiza, etc.)

Step 3: Upload enriched numbers

  1. Switch to the Save New Numbers tab
  2. Select the client you enriched for
  3. Paste the Google Sheet URL of your enriched list (must have a Phone column)
  4. Click Save Numbers to Database
  5. The new numbers are added to the master database and linked to the client

Phone number formatting

No need to clean up phone numbers or rename columns before uploading. The tool automatically detects any column containing "phone", "mobile", "cell", or "tel" in the header (e.g. Phone Number 1, Mobile Number, Cell Phone). If there are multiple phone columns, it picks the first valid number for each contact. All numbers are automatically formatted to international standard (+digits only), regardless of how they come from Apollo, Clay, or Wiza. Spaces, brackets, dashes, and other special characters are all stripped. Invalid or junk values are skipped.
